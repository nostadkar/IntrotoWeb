Restaurant Description

We are a family owned and operated old school pizzeria. Our pizza has a sweet sauce and buttery crust. Kids are totally welcome at Ava's. But, if you are looking for a beer, glass of wine, or a cocktail, we have those too. Our party room holds 30 to 100 people and is a great place to celebrate special occasions. We are located in historic, downtown area across the street from the Bus Station. Stop in and check us out.
